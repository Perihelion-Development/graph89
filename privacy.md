# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use **Graph 89** (the “App”).

---

## Overview

Your privacy is important. Graph 89 is designed to function without collecting or storing personal user data. The only data processed relates to anonymous crash and stability analytics, used solely to improve app reliability.

---

## Information Collection and Use

### Personal Data

Graph 89 **does not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location data
- Device identifiers linked to an individual

---

## Crash Analytics

Graph 89 uses crash reporting tools provided by the Android platform or third-party services (such as Google Play or Firebase Crash Reporting) to identify and fix software defects.

These tools may collect:

- App crash logs
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Technical information related to the crash

This information:

- Is **anonymous**
- Is **not used to identify users**
- Is used **only for debugging and improving app stability**

---

## Data Sharing

No user data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Crash analytics data may be processed by trusted service providers solely for error reporting and diagnostics.

---

## Data Security

Because Graph 89 does not collect personal data, there is no stored personal information at risk. Crash analytics data is handled according to the security practices of the analytics provider.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Graph 89 does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, with the updated date listed at the top.
